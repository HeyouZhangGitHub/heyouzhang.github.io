Brief Biography

Heyou Zhang - Ph.D. in Chemistry
heyouz@student.unimelb.edu.au

  • Learn More

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the ARC Centre of Excellence in Exciton Science, working at NanoScience Laboratory (Prof. Paul Mulvaney), School of Chemistry at the University of Melbourne, Australia. I received my B.Sc. (2013) in Chemistry from Wuhan University, China, and M.Sc. (2016) in Chemistry from the University of Melbourne, Australia.

My research interests are focused on Nano Science, Nanocrystal Synthesis, Nanofabrication and Assembly, Single Crystal Spectroscopy and Catalysis.
I am also on Google Scholar ORCID ACEx ResearchGate
Beside science, I am interested in Photography, Travelling, Cocktail and Coffee, and Sports.

My Research Interests

Nanoscience - Nanocrystals.
Preparation, Fabrication, Characterization, Application.

  • Learn More

I have wide interests in the study of Nanoscience. From the nanocrystals synthesis and functionalizaion. To single nanocrystal assembly and fabrication. In addtion with the study of optical and electrical properites. Plus basic theory study and numerical simulation. End up with the nanotechnologies in catalysis and sensing.

My current research is focused on the Direct Assembly of Single Nanocrystal Arrays by Electrophoretic Depostion.
